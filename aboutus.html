Our Currency Prediction Model

We use three different approaches; Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM), AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average (ARIMA), and Exponential Smoothing (ETS).

Our model is unique because it combines multiple techniques, providing a more comprehensive approach to currency forecasting. We have strong confidence in the accuracy of our model and have seen significant success in predicting the Cambodian Riel exchange rate.

Our Vision

Our mission at Currency Prediction Model is to inspire Cambodian people to take a greater interest in finance and technology, specifically the application of data science and machine learning in forecasting. We believe that everyone can benefit from a better understanding of these fields, and our model is a great example of how they can be combined to create practical solutions that benefit society.
